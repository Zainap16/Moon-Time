Welcome to Moon-Time!

A woman's body is constantly changing. Different women change in different ways, therefore it can often times be confusing and you are left feeling alone.

Moon-Time is designed to answer the majority questions women, especially young girls, may have concerning their period and the small discrepencies from the norm.

It's important to keep in mind that school education caters to common asked questions and that some ladies may differ in terms of period duration, flow and hormonal balance. Some may be harmless, however their are certain things that is better addressed by a doctor.
