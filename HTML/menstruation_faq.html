When can I expect to have my first period?

The age at which individuals experience their first period, known as menarche, varies, usually occurring between 9 to 16 years old. It can be influenced by genetics, nutrition, and overall health.

How long does a period typically last?

A menstrual period usually lasts 3 to 7 days. However, the duration can vary, and periods shorter or longer than this range can still be considered normal.

How can I manage menstrual cramps and discomfort?

To manage menstrual cramps and discomfort, you can use over-the-counter pain relievers, apply heat to the lower abdomen, practice relaxation techniques, and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Is it normal to experience mood swings and emotional changes during my cycle?

Yes, it's normal to experience mood swings and emotional changes due to hormonal fluctuations during the menstrual cycle.

How do I track my menstrual cycle?

You can track your menstrual cycle using a calendar, a period-tracking app, or a menstrual cycle diary. Record the start and end dates of your period each month to understand your cycle better.
